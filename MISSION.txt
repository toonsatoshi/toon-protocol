# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Toon is a decentralized music ownership platform built natively on The Open Network (TON) and operated entirely through Telegram. Artists upload tracks, mint them as on-chain NFTs, and earn directly from fans through a social-first token economy powered by $TOON. Fans discover music, tip artists, refer friends, and invest in fractional music royalties — all without leaving the messaging app they already use every day.

Toon is not Audius on TON. Toon is what happens when you build a music platform from the inside of a 1-billion-user social network instead of trying to drag those users to a new one.

The platform is currently in active development with a functional Telegram bot (@ToonPlayerBot), seven smart contracts deployed on TON testnet, a working $TOON Jetton token, persistent user storage, referral mechanics, and native audio playback fully operational. This document serves as both a business proposal for potential partners and investors, and an internal product directive for continued development.

# THE PROBLEM

**For Artists:**
The streaming economy is broken. Spotify pays between $0.003 and $0.005 per stream. An independent artist needs approximately 250,000 streams per month to earn minimum wage. The platforms that distribute their work own the relationship with their fans. Discovery is algorithmic and opaque. Upfront funding for recording and promotion is inaccessible without label deals that extract the majority of long-term value. Web3 music platforms promised a fix but delivered governance tokens that dropped 97% from their all-time highs and micro-royalties measured in fractions of a cent.

**For Fans:**
Music consumption is passive and economically disconnected. You stream a billion streams of an artist before they're famous and receive nothing when they blow up. There is no mechanism to invest in an artist you believe in before the rest of the world catches on. Tipping exists on some platforms but is frictionless only in the sense that it's meaningless — no economic skin in the game, no social recognition, no return.

**For the Web3 Music Space:**
Every previous attempt — Audius, Catalog, Sound.xyz, Royal.io, Anotherblock — required users to adopt new behavior, download new apps, connect new wallets, and navigate new interfaces. They built crypto platforms that hoped musicians and fans would come. None of them started where the people already were.

# THE SOLUTION: TOON

Toon starts where 1 billion people already are.

Telegram is not the distribution channel for Toon. Telegram IS Toon. The bot is the app. The chat is the interface. The wallet is already in the phone. The notification infrastructure is the blockchain's native voice. When a fan tips an artist on Toon, the artist receives a Telegram notification. Not an email. Not an app ping. A message — in the same place they talk to their friends.

This is only possible on TON. No other blockchain is architecturally fused with a messaging platform at this scale. TON was built by Telegram's original founders. The integration is not a partnership — it is the design.

**Toon solves three things simultaneously:**

1. **Artist monetization** — 95%+ of tips go directly to the artist wallet. No intermediaries. No payout thresholds. No 30-day delays.

2. **Fan investment** — ToonDrops allow fans to purchase fractional royalty rights to tracks they believe in. When the track earns, they earn. Automatically. On-chain.

3. **Social distribution** — $TOON flows for social actions, not passive consumption. Referring a friend who uploads earns more than a thousand passive plays. The token economy incentivizes human behavior that actually grows the platform.

# PRODUCT OVERVIEW

## Current State (Testnet — April 2026)

**@ToonPlayerBot** is live on Telegram with the following working features:

- **Onboarding** — Custom artist/listener name selection. Referral code attribution at signup. Off-chain pending identity until first track upload, then on-chain ToonArtist contract deploys.
- **Audio Playback** — Native Telegram audio player with correct title and performer metadata. Tracks play inline in chat identically to a friend sending a song.
- **Artist Discovery** — Browse all artists on Toon, select an artist, see their full track catalog with play counts.
- **Play Tracking** — Every play increments the track's on-chain reputation counter. No $TOON for plays — reputation only. Anti-farming by design.
- **Tipping** — Send TON directly to an artist's ToonTrack contract address. Superfan rebate (8% in $TOON) for tips of 100+ TON.
- **Referral System** — Unique referral codes generated at signup. Reward chain: +5 $TOON at friend signup, +25 $TOON when friend uploads, +50 $TOON when friend's track hits 5 unique plays.
- **Sharing** — Shareable deep links that open directly to a track in bot with Play and Tip buttons.
- **Persistent Storage** — db.json survives bot restarts. Full user profiles, track catalogs, referral chains.
- **Profile** — Reputation score, $TOON balance, track list with play counts, referral stats, on-chain identity status.

## Smart Contract Architecture (TON Testnet)

Seven contracts deployed and monitored via TON Notify Bot (Telegram-native contract monitoring):

**ToonRegistry** — Global source of truth. Every artist and track must register here. Enforces uniqueness. Controls $TOON mint authority.

**ToonArtist** — One per artist. Permanent on-chain identity. Stores Telegram handle hash, reputation, tip volume, staked $TOON, and track addresses. Reputation gates unlock capabilities — low reputation can upload, high reputation can launch ToonDrops.

**ToonTrack** — One per song. Core NFT. Stores artist address, Arweave metadata URI, ACRCloud audio fingerprint hash (copyright layer), mint fee, and reputation counter. Accepts TON tips directly. Forwards value to artist. Emits $TOON to tipper from vault reserve.

**ToonTip** — Advanced tipping contract. Split tips across multiple tracks in one transaction. Recurring subscriptions. Group tip pools — group collectively funds a tip, all names attached to the on-chain event.

**ToonVault** — Reward engine. Daily emission hard cap. Automatic halving when reserve drops below 10%. Self-recovering when reserve exceeds 20%. Sybil protection via wallet age requirement. Processes all seven reward categories with multipliers.

**ToonGovernance** — $TOON holders vote on protocol parameters. Stake-weighted and duration-weighted voting power. Passed proposals execute automatically via message to Registry and Vault. No multisig, no team intervention.

**ToonJettonMaster / ToonJettonWallet** — $TOON token. Standard TON Jetton with mint authority designed to lock to ToonVault exclusively. Decimals: 9. Symbol: TOON.

**Planned:**
- **ToonDrop** — RWA fractional royalty contract. Artist offers percentage of track royalties. Fans buy slices with $TOON. Auto-distributes when vault pushes royalty payments.
- **ToonFan** — Artist-specific fan tokens. Gates exclusive content, early drop access, artist-level governance votes.

# BUSINESS MODEL

Toon generates revenue through multiple non-extractive mechanisms:

**1. ToonDrop Platform Fee (3%)**
When an artist launches a ToonDrop and raises capital from fans, Toon takes 3% of the raise into the Treasury. At scale, a single successful drop from an artist with 10,000 engaged fans could represent meaningful treasury inflow.

**2. $TOON Purchase Rails (spread/fee)**
Three rails are planned:
- Telegram Stars → $TOON (built into BotFather, zero external setup)
- TON → $TOON swap (send TON to treasury address, receive $TOON at protocol rate)
- Fiat card → $TOON (BotFather native payment providers — Redsys and others already available)

A small spread on each purchase goes to the Treasury.

**3. Featured Placement (paid in $TOON, partially burned)**
Artists pay $TOON to be featured in the discovery interface, on weekly charts, or as the default track in the /listen flow. A portion burns, a portion goes to Treasury.

**4. Verification Tiers**
Artists pay $TOON to unlock verification badges (Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum) based on reputation thresholds. Verification unlocks higher ToonDrop raise limits, featured placement eligibility, and early access to new platform features.

**5. ToonFan Token Launch Fee**
When an artist's reputation unlocks ToonFan and they launch their own fan token, Toon takes a small percentage of the initial distribution.

**Treasury:**
All revenue flows to a dedicated Treasury wallet. Treasury allocations are voted on by $TOON holders via ToonGovernance. Proposals can fund development, marketing, artist acquisition programs, or liquidity.

# PRODUCT IDEAS — THE TOON-TASTIC EXPANSION PACK

These are features that don't exist yet but belong in the product vision:

## 🎯 Toon Curators
A whole new user class beyond artists and fans. Curators stake $TOON on artists they believe in before those artists are famous. If the artist's reputation crosses thresholds, the curator earns a share proportional to how early they staked. This is music A&R on-chain. It creates a third economic actor — the tastemaker — and gives people with great taste but no music-making ability a way to earn from their knowledge. The on-chain social graph makes this verifiable. You can see who staked on an artist before they blew up. That's a reputation that compounds forever.

## 📻 Toon Radio Stations
Community-curated playlists backed by $TOON stakes. Anyone can start a radio station by staking $TOON. The station has a name, a genre, a description. Artists submit tracks for inclusion. Station owners curate. When listeners play a track from a station, a micro-tip flows to the station owner in addition to the artist. Stations compete for listeners. Good curation earns. Bad curation loses stakers. This is Spotify playlist curators but economically aligned and on-chain.

## 🎪 Toon Live
Telegram has native voice chats in channels. Artists go live in their channel. Toon bot monitors the live session. Fans tip in real-time during the performance — the bot announces each tip in the chat like a DJ shoutout. The artist's reputation increments in real-time as tips flow. When the session ends, the bot posts a summary: total tips received, top supporters, session replay if recorded. This is a live concert inside a chat app. Nothing like this exists anywhere.

## 🤝 Toon Collabs
Two or more artists co-mint a track together. The ToonTrack contract splits tip revenue automatically at whatever percentage the artists agree to at mint time. The track appears in both artists' profiles. Both fan communities see it. Tips from either community split to both wallets. Collaborations drive cross-community discovery without any algorithmic recommendation needed. The social graph handles it.

## 🏆 Toon Seasons
Quarterly competitive cycles. Each season has a leaderboard — top artists by tip volume, top listeners by streak, top curators by staked-artist performance. At the end of each season, special season-exclusive $TOON rewards distribute to top performers, season NFT badges mint to participants, and the charts reset. This creates recurring hype cycles every 90 days without requiring new feature launches. The gamification is the feature.

## 🎫 Toon Moments
NFT clips — 15 to 30 seconds of a track — minted as collectible moments. A fan who loved an artist before they were famous can mint the moment of first discovery. Limited edition. Provably early. The on-chain timestamp proves when you were there. As the artist's reputation grows, the early Moments become artifacts. This is cultural provenance on-chain. "I have the Moment from their first track before they had 1,000 listeners." That's worth something to the right fan.

## 🏷️ Toon for Labels
White-label version of the Toon infrastructure for indie labels to manage their entire roster. The label gets a branded bot, a custom token if desired, and access to the ToonDrop machinery to raise capital for their artists. Toon takes a platform fee. Labels pay for the infrastructure. Artists benefit from the label's promotional muscle plus the on-chain royalty transparency. This is B2B revenue on top of the consumer platform.

## 📊 Toon Intelligence
The social graph is the product but it's also data. Aggregated and anonymized: which genres generate the most tip volume, which referral chains produce the most active users, which track durations retain listeners longest, which artist onboarding paths convert casual listeners to $TOON holders. This intelligence is valuable to artists, labels, and eventually to music industry players trying to understand the Web3 audience. Not sold — used to improve the platform and offered as premium analytics to artists above certain reputation thresholds.

## 🔐 Toon Verified Artist
Connect your Spotify, SoundCloud, or Apple Music profile to Toon and prove you're a real artist with an established catalog. Verified artists get a checkmark, a higher reputation ceiling, and eligibility for ToonDrop at lower thresholds. This creates a bridge between the existing music industry and the on-chain economy, gives real artists a reason to migrate their audience to Toon, and creates a trust signal that listeners can rely on when investing in ToonDrops.

## 🌍 Toon Global Communities
Music is global. Telegram is global. $TOON is global. Toon should have language-specific discovery channels — a Spanish-language bot experience, a Portuguese one, a French one — not separate platforms but localized onboarding flows that route into the same protocol. Latin music alone is the fastest-growing music genre in the world. K-pop fandoms on Telegram are among the most financially engaged communities in existence. These communities don't need convincing to support their artists financially — they need a mechanism. Toon is the mechanism.

## 🎙️ Toon Podcast
The exact same infrastructure — upload, mint, tip, refer, earn — works for podcasters. Audio file goes up, fans tip for episodes they loved, curators stake on shows they believe in, advertisers pay in $TOON for placement in highly-tipped episodes. The TAM for podcasting is enormous and completely underserved by Web3. Adding podcasting support doubles the addressable creator market with zero additional smart contract development.
